About ReWorth

Waste is a design flaw. AI helps us fix it.

The linear "take, make, dispose" model has run its course. ReWorth reframes waste as a resource — using computer vision and intelligent reasoning to close the loop between what we discard and what we could reuse.

The circular economy problem

Every year, billions of tonnes of material are extracted, used once, and thrown away. Recoverable value is lost at the exact point where a small decision — reuse, recycle, or repurpose — could have kept it in circulation.

Why AI changes the equation

Manual sorting is slow, inconsistent, and hard to scale. A trained model can recognize material types instantly and consistently — turning waste segregation from a guessing game into a precise, repeatable decision.

IDENTIFY

Sees what it's looking at

ReWorth's model analyzes an image of discarded material and classifies its type and condition in real time.

RECOMMEND

Suggests the next best use

Based on the classification, it recommends whether the item is best reused, recycled, or repurposed — and how.

IMPACT

Feeds a cleaner loop

Every correct decision keeps material in circulation longer, cutting waste and building toward a more sustainable future.
